📖 How it Works & Guide

This tool is designed to be fast, private, and easy to use. All calculations and processing happen directly in your browser.

  1. Input: Enter your data or upload the required file into the designated input area.
  2. Process: Click the primary action button to trigger the instant calculation or conversion.
  3. Result: Review the output and use the copy button to save your results to the clipboard.
