📖 How to Use This Calculator

TDEE (Total Daily Energy Expenditure) is the total number of calories your body burns in a day including exercise. This is your maintenance calorie level.

  1. Select Gender: Choose male or female for accurate BMR calculation.
  2. Enter Stats: Input your age, weight (kg), and height (cm).
  3. Activity Level: Select how active you are on a weekly basis.
  4. Read Results: See your maintenance, cutting (-500), and bulking (+500) calorie targets.

Common Use Cases

  • Setting calorie targets for fat loss (eat below TDEE)
  • Planning a muscle-building diet (eat above TDEE)
  • Understanding how activity level affects daily calorie needs
  • Meal planning for competitive athletes
Pro Tip: For safe weight loss, aim for a 500 calorie deficit (1 lb/week). Never go below 1,200 calories for women or 1,500 for men without medical supervision.
