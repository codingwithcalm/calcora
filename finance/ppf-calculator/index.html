❓ Frequently Asked Questions
Is PPF completely tax-free?

Yes! PPF enjoys EEE (Exempt-Exempt-Exempt) status. Your investment qualifies for 80C deduction, the interest earned is tax-free, and the maturity amount is also tax-free. It's one of the very few instruments with this triple benefit.

Can I extend PPF beyond 15 years?

Yes. After 15 years, you can extend in blocks of 5 years (with or without fresh contributions). Extended PPF continues to earn interest at the prevailing rate.

Can NRIs open a PPF account?

NRIs cannot open new PPF accounts. However, existing accounts opened as a resident can be continued until maturity (15 years). Extension is not allowed for NRIs.

How much will ₹1.5L/year PPF give after 15 years?

At the current rate of 7.1%: Total invested = ₹22.5L, Maturity amount = ₹40.68L, Tax-free interest = ₹18.18L. This is one of the safest ways to double your money.
