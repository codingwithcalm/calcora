📖 How to Use This Calculator

Convert your annual salary to an hourly wage in seconds. Perfect for comparing job offers, negotiating pay, or understanding your true earning rate.

  1. Enter Salary: Type your annual base salary (before taxes) in the salary field.
  2. Set Hours: Adjust hours worked per week (default 40 for full-time).
  3. Set Weeks: Change weeks per year if you have unpaid time off (default 52).
  4. Read Results: Your hourly, daily, weekly, and monthly wages update instantly.

Common Use Cases

  • Comparing a salaried position vs an hourly contract offer
  • Freelancers setting their hourly billing rate based on desired annual income
  • Understanding overtime pay implications
  • Budgeting based on actual take-home per period
Pro Tip: If you get 2 weeks unpaid vacation, set weeks to 50 instead of 52 — this gives a more accurate hourly rate since you're only paid for 50 weeks.
