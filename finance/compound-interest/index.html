📖 How to Use This Calculator

See the power of compounding in action. This calculator shows how your initial investment plus regular monthly contributions grow over time.

  1. Initial Investment: Enter the amount you have today to invest.
  2. Monthly Contribution: Set how much you can add each month (even $0 works).
  3. Years: Choose your investment time horizon.
  4. Interest Rate: Enter the expected annual return percentage.

Common Use Cases

  • Projecting retirement savings growth over 20-30 years
  • Comparing fixed deposit rates vs stock market returns
  • Showing the impact of starting to invest early vs late
  • Planning for a child's education fund
Pro Tip: The "Rule of 72" is a quick way to estimate doubling time. Divide 72 by your annual return rate. At 7%, your money doubles roughly every ~10.3 years.
