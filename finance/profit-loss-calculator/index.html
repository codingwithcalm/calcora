📖 Guide: Margin vs Markup

Understanding the difference between markup and margin is critical for accurate pricing and financial planning.

Margin = (Profit ÷ Selling Price) × 100
Markup = (Profit ÷ Cost Price) × 100

Example: If you buy a product for ₹50 and sell it for ₹100, your profit is ₹50.
Your Markup is 100% (50/50), but your Margin is 50% (50/100).
