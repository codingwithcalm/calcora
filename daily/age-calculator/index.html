❓ Frequently Asked Questions
How does this calculator handle leap years?

The calculator automatically accounts for leap years (years divisible by 4, except centuries not divisible by 400). February 29 is correctly handled for people born on that date.

What age is considered a senior citizen in India?

In India, a person aged 60 years and above is classified as a senior citizen. Super senior citizens (80+) get additional tax benefits under the Income Tax Act.

How accurate is this age calculator?

This calculator is accurate to the exact day. It uses standardized date arithmetic that accounts for varying month lengths (28-31 days) and leap years.

Can I calculate the age difference between two people?

Yes! Enter one person's birthdate and use the other person's birthdate as the target date. The result shows the exact age difference in years, months, and days.

What is the legal voting age worldwide?

Most countries set voting age at 18 (including India, US, UK, EU). Some exceptions: Argentina and Brazil allow voting at 16, while in Japan and South Korea it's 18 (recently lowered from 19-20).
