📘 How to Use

This scientific calculator supports standard arithmetic as well as advanced mathematical functions. All processing happens locally on your device for total privacy.

  • Basic Math: Use the number pad and operators (+, -, ×, ÷) for simple calculations.
  • Trigonometry: Use sin, cos, and tan (calculates in degrees).
  • Logarithms: 'log' for base 10 and 'ln' for natural logarithms.
  • Memory: 'MS' saves the current value, 'MR' recalls it into your expression.
