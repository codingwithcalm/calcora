📖 How to Use This Calculator

Quickly calculate the perfect tip and split the bill among friends. Works for restaurants, salons, delivery, and any service.

  1. Enter Bill: Type the total bill amount before tip.
  2. Choose Tip: Select 15%, 18%, 20%, or enter a custom percentage.
  3. Split: Set the number of people to split the bill with.
  4. Read Results: See per-person amount, total tip, and total bill instantly.

Tipping Etiquette Guide

  • 15% — Standard for acceptable service
  • 18% — Good service (most common default)
  • 20% — Excellent service or fine dining
  • 25%+ — Exceptional service or large parties
Pro Tip: A quick mental math trick for 20% — move the decimal one place left (10%) and double it. A $85 bill = $8.50 x 2 = $17 tip.
